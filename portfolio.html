  • 2018-2017 - First coordinator of Ukrainian Academy Film Awards GOLDEN DZIGA, Kyiv.   • 2017-2018 - Organiser of the Ukrainian Academy Education Programme, Kyiv. – This project connected experienced film directors with film students for the purpose of mentoring and sharing experience.
